Welcome to The Space Place! Here, we Earthings bring you comfort food to the mystical shores of Venus! We know many of you may be new here, so let us help you feel at home!

Founded in 2023, The Space Place began with a simple mission: Bring the classic flavors of Earthling food to the newfound shores of Venus. We have crafted our menu around iconic flavors and only the finest ingredients sourced from all over the Milky Way.

Check out our great selection of classic delicacies, from mozzarella sticks, to Venus grown tomatoes in our caprese salad. Try any of our delicious offerings to be blasted off into a world full of incredible flavor.
